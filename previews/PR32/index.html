Features

Qaintensor.jl is an extension to the digital quantum circuit toolbox and simulator Qaintessent.jl. This library allows the conversion of Circuit/CircuitGateChain objects created in Qaintessent.jl into TensorNetwork objects. (See Tensors for details regarding conversion and the creation of general TensorNetworks). Qaintensor.jl currently supports MPS and MPO states, see MPS Representation and MPO Representation.
